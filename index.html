Principal's Message

The Woodstock School, founded in 1985 by Mrs. Saroj & Dr. R.P. Jaiswal, is marching ahead and has completed thirty five years of its glorious existence. It has established itself as a leading and prime institution of Buxar and has committed and dedicated itself to the cause of imparting value based education.

Our aim has always been to emphasize on virtues of the students and the quality of education that is provided – hence we have always limited the number of students, as we believe that only when the numbers are limited, there can be a personal rapport between teachers and students. We have made untiring efforts to make education a pleasure and not a pain for the students and to remove the fear psychosis that is normally instilled and to create a healthy, happy and joyous environment where the young minds can grow and be creative.

We aim at the all-round development of the child and wish to improve and nurture all aspects of his/her personality. Our education system is modern and apart from academics, computer, co-curricular activities & sports are also given importance.

The students of Woodstock School are adaptive, creative, bright, balanced and happy. Our teachers are well educated, loving and understanding. We realize and want others also to realize that education is not a degree but it is a way of life and true education is what remains ingrained in you when all that was learnt is forgotten.

Thus our effort is to truly enlighten our students with the light of knowledge and learning because as the school motto says: “Knowledge Enlightens”

Rajay Jaiswal
Principal
