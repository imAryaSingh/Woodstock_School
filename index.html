Welcome to Woodstock School

Nurturing Excellence in Education

Principal's Message

Welcome to Woodstock School, where we believe in shaping young minds to become responsible citizens of tomorrow. Our faculty is dedicated to creating a nurturing and challenging learning environment.

Aims & Objectives

We believe that a school is a building with four walls and tomorrow inside it. We therefore strive for excellence in all vistas.

  • At providing quality education.
  • To propagate patriotic & correct social & ethical values.
  • To ensure proper physical, mental & moral growth of students and to create in them a yearning and zest for learning.
  • To encourage team work & cooperation.
  • To create a special & strong bond of mutual respect & understanding between the students and teachers.
  • To work and create an environment free from fear or favour.
  • Thus the quintessence of our efforts is to make students well read, sensible, balanced & patriotic individuals who can shoulder all responsibilities that the society or nation may place upon them.
Academic Excellence

To foster a culture of learning and critical thinking.

Global Citizenship

Encouraging awareness and appreciation for global cultures and values.

Character Building

Instilling values of integrity, empathy, and discipline.
